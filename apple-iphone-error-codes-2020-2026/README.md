# Apple iPhone Error Codes (2020–2026)
Reference list of common iPhone diagnostic and restore error codes for models released between 2020 and 2026.

## Sample Error Codes
- 4013 – Restore Error
- 4014 – Hardware Communication Error
- 9 – Device Disconnect Error
- 56 – Touch IC Failure
- 1110 – Secure Enclave Error

## Covered iPhone Models (2020–2026)
- iPhone SE (2020)
- iPhone 12 Series (2020)
- iPhone 13 Series (2021)
- iPhone 14 Series (2022)
- iPhone 15 Series (2023)
- iPhone 16 Series (2024)
- iPhone 17 Series (2025)
- iPhone 18 Series (2026, future-ready)
